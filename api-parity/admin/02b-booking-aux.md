# Doi chieu API & nghiep vu — LO 02b: Booking phu tro

Pham vi: lich su log, tai khoan ngan hang khach, giao xe, gia han, nhan xe.

- **So route.ts web da quet:** 6 (booking-logs/get, customer-bank-account, delivery/perform, delivery/search, extend/create, receive/perform).
- **Tong so dong doi chieu:** 7 (6 hanh dong web + 1 dong APP_ONLY: app goi truc tiep booking/detail trong luong giao xe).
- **Tom tat muc do:** CRITICAL = 2 | HIGH = 3 | MEDIUM = 1 | OK/LOW = phan con lai.

> Ghi chu chung ve responseCode:
> - Web: route.ts (lop BFF) chi forward theo HTTP status; viec kiem `responseCode === "00"` nam o lop hook FE (useDeliveryReceive, useExtend...). => Web CO kiem "00" cho perform-delivery / perform-receive / extend.
> - App Flutter: TAT CA service chi kiem `statusCode == 200`, KHONG kiem `responseCode == "00"`. => Neu BE tra HTTP 200 nhung responseCode = "09" (loi nghiep vu), app van bao "thanh cong". Day la lech he thong, anh huong nhieu dong ben duoi.

## Diem can luu y cho lead (mo ta nghiep vu)

1. **Giao xe & Nhan xe tren app dang gui sai ten o tien** (CRITICAL): App va web dat ten cac o "tien coc thuc thu", "tien thue thu them", "phu phi" khac nhau. Neu BE chi hieu ten cua web, thi khi nhan vien dung app bam Giao xe / Nhan xe, mot so khoan tien co the khong duoc ghi nhan dung => sai so lieu thu/chi. Can doi BE xac nhan ten field nao la chuan.
2. **App gui anh gia khi Nhan xe** (CRITICAL): Man Nhan xe tren app chua lam chuc nang upload anh that, no tu chen 1 anh gia ten `dummy-image-url` de "qua" rang buoc bat buoc co anh. => Hop dong tren app se co anh hong/khong xem duoc.
3. **App khong canh bao khi BE bao loi nghiep vu** (anh huong nhieu chuc nang): App chi nhin HTTP 200 de ket luan thanh cong; web thi con kiem ma "00". Nen co the xay ra: BE tu choi (vd sai trang thai) nhung app van bao "thanh cong".
4. **App thieu chuc nang xem STK ngan hang cua khach** (HIGH): Khi can hoan coc, web cho xem so tai khoan ngan hang khach; app khong co.
5. **App gia han khong cho sua tong tien** (HIGH): Web cho nhap tay tong tien moi khi gia han va tu huy yeu cau hoan coc neu dang cho; app chi doi ngay ket thuc.
